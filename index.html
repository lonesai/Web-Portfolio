I am a Software Developer from New York City

Growing up in a city like New York, I’ve always been surrounded by the best technologies the world had to offer. However it wasn’t the amazing science in the tech that pulled me to this industry, but rather the way people chose to use that tech in creative and meaningful ways. Seeing first hand that technology can be influenced by music, art and culture in general, lit a fire in me that led me to dedicate my career to.

Education

I am a software engineering student currently studying computer science and mathematics at the University at Buffalo. Over the past few years I have been working on a variety of independent projects alongside my formal education at university.

Through these experiences, I have worked with many different languages like Java, C and Python, as well as the latest web frameworks and API’s to build responsive and intuitive web apps. However I know my education doesn’t stop here, I will strive to learn and grow as a developer to keep up with the ever changing technology.

Passion for Art and Design

My love for aesthetic design for UI/UX and passion for functionality and structure is the basis for my development process. I believe software development is one of the most powerful tools to solve challenging problems, but I also believe that these tools, if used in creative ways can, be interpreted as art. My goal as a developer is to find the best balance between these two notions or even connect them as one.
